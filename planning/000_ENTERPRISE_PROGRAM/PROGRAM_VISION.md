# Enterprise Program Vision

## Program Identity
**Name:** MeterVerse Enterprise Platform  
**Type:** Multi-product enterprise utility platform  
**Products:** System A (Admin), System B (User Workspace)  
**Status:** Wave 01 Complete — Scaling toward Enterprise Release  

## Vision Statement
To become the definitive enterprise utility management platform — unifying meter data management, billing, customer engagement, and operational intelligence under a single architecture that scales across organizations, regions, and regulatory environments.

## Strategic Objectives
1. **Unified Platform** — One architecture serving both admin and user portals
2. **Enterprise Grade** — Security, performance, compliance, and reliability at scale
3. **Multi-Product** — Support MeterVerse Admin, User Workspace, and future products
4. **AI-Native** — Intelligence embedded at every layer, not bolted on
5. **Global Ready** — Multi-tenancy, multi-currency, multi-language, multi-regulation

## Product Lines
| Product | Purpose | Users | Status |
|---------|---------|-------|--------|
| MeterVerse Admin (System A) | Enterprise administration, configuration, billing engine | Internal staff, administrators | Wave 01 Complete |
| MeterVerse User (System B) | Daily operational work, customer/meter/reading management | Field operators, customer service | Wave 01 Complete |
| MeterVerse Mobile (Future) | Field data collection, offline readings | Field technicians | Wave 06 Planned |
| MeterVerse Partner API (Future) | Third-party integration, white-label billing | Partners, resellers | Not yet planned |

## Enterprise Principles
1. **Shared Core** — Every product shares auth, database, runtime, and business rules
2. **Separation by Scope** — Admin configures, User operates, Mobile collects
3. **Permission-First** — Every action requires an explicit permission key
4. **Graph-Verified** — Every change must update the knowledge graph
5. **Spec-Validated** — Every step must pass SpecKit before completion
