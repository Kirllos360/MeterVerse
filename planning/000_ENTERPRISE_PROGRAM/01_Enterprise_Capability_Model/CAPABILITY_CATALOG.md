# Enterprise Capability Model

**Purpose:** Master catalog of all business capabilities. Every feature maps to a capability.

| Capability | Description | Sub-Capabilities | Owner | Wave |
|-----------|-------------|-----------------|-------|------|
| Customer Management | End-to-end customer lifecycle management | Customer CRUD, Customer Timeline, Customer Documents, Customer Billing, Customer Notification, Customer Contracts, Customer Analytics, Customer AI | System A + B | Wave 01 |
| Meter Management | Meter inventory, assignment, and lifecycle | Meter CRUD, Meter Assignment, Meter Events, Meter Reading Collection, Meter Analytics | System A + B | Wave 01 |
| Reading Management | Consumption data ingestion and validation | Reading CRUD, Bulk Import, Validation Rules, Anomaly Detection, Reading Analytics | System A + B | Wave 01 + Wave 05 |
| Billing Management | Invoice generation, tariff application, payment processing | Tariff Engine, BillRun Engine, Invoice Generation, Discount Engine, Tax Engine, Payment Processing, Collections | System A | Wave 01 + Wave 03 |
| Payment Management | Payment collection, reconciliation, and reporting | Payment CRUD, Payment Gateway, Payment Reconciliation, Promise-to-Pay, Collection Workflow | System A + B | Wave 01 + Wave 03 |
| Contract Management | Customer contract lifecycle and terms | Contract CRUD, Contract Terms, Contract Amendments, Contract Expiry Notifications, Auto-Renewal | System A | Wave 01 |
| Notification Management | Multi-channel communication with customers and staff | In-App Notifications, Email Notifications, SMS Notifications, Push Notifications, Notification Templates, Notification Preferences | System A + B | Wave 01 + Wave 02 |
| User Management | User accounts, roles, permissions, and authentication | User CRUD, Role Management, Permission Management, Authentication, Session Management, MFA, Audit Trail | System A | Wave 01 |
| Reporting & Analytics | Business intelligence and KPI tracking | Report Definitions, Scheduled Reports, KPI Dashboard, Real-Time Dashboard, Custom Report Builder, Data Export | System A | Wave 01 + Wave 05 |
| System Administration | Platform configuration, monitoring, and maintenance | System Settings, Feature Flags, Audit Log, Monitoring, Backup/Restore, Cache Management, Queue Management | System A | Wave 01 + Wave 04 |
