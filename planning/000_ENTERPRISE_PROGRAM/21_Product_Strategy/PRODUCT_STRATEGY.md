# Product Strategy

**Purpose:** The "WHY" layer — why every feature exists and what business outcome it drives.

## North Star Metric
**Bills Paid On Time** — The single metric that indicates overall platform health.

## Strategic Pillars

| Pillar | Objective | Key Results |
|--------|-----------|-------------|
| Operational Excellence | Zero unplanned downtime | 99.9% uptime, < 1hr incident response |
| Customer Success | Faster billing cycles | Bill within 24h of reading, 98% on-time payment |
| Employee Productivity | Reduce manual work | 50% reduction in manual data entry |
| Financial Accuracy | Zero billing errors | 99.99% billing accuracy, automated reconciliation |
| Scalability | Global readiness | Support 1M+ customers, 10+ organizations |

## Target Markets
1. Utility companies (water, electricity, gas)
2. Property management firms
3. Sub-metering operators
4. Energy resellers

## Competitive Advantage
1. Unified admin + user platform (vs. fragmented tools)
2. AI-native anomaly detection (vs. reactive alerts)
3. Open API ecosystem (vs. closed systems)
4. Multi-tenant by design (vs. single-tenant legacy)
