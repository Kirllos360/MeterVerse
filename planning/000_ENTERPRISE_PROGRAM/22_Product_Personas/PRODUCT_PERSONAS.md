# Product Personas

**Purpose:** Every feature is built for a persona. If no persona needs it, don't build it.

## Personas

### Admin (System A)
- **Role:** System administrator, billing manager
- **Needs:** Configure tariffs, run billing, manage users, view reports
- **Pain Points:** Manual billing processes, data inconsistency, lack of visibility
- **Success Criteria:** Bills generated accurately and on time

### Operator (System B)
- **Role:** Field operator, customer service rep
- **Needs:** Read meters, manage customers, view invoices, record payments
- **Pain Points:** Paper-based processes, data entry errors, slow customer lookup
- **Success Criteria:** Complete daily reading targets, resolve customer issues in one call

### Finance Manager
- **Role:** Accounts receivable, financial controller
- **Needs:** Payment reconciliation, aging reports, revenue forecasts
- **Pain Points:** Late payments, reconciliation effort, cash flow visibility
- **Success Criteria:** Reduce DSO (Days Sales Outstanding) by 30%

### Customer
- **Role:** End customer receiving bills
- **Needs:** View bills, make payments, view consumption, submit readings
- **Pain Points:** Complicated bills, limited payment options, no usage insights
- **Success Criteria:** Bills paid on time, self-service adoption > 60%

### Executive
- **Role:** CEO, COO, CFO
- **Needs:** Dashboards, KPIs, trend analysis, compliance reports
- **Pain Points:** Data silos, slow reporting, lack of predictive insights
- **Success Criteria:** Data-driven decisions, regulatory compliance
