# MeterVerse — Project Vision

## Elevator Pitch
MeterVerse is an Enterprise Utility Operating System that manages utility meter infrastructure across multiple sites — from customer onboarding through meter installation, reading collection, billing, payments, and collections — in one unified platform.

## Strategic Objectives
1. **Unified Platform**: Single pane of glass for water, electricity, and gas metering
2. **Enterprise Grade**: Role-based access, multi-tenant, audit trails, soft delete
3. **Real-time**: Live monitoring with alerting and anomaly detection
4. **Automated**: Billing, reporting, and workflow automation
5. **Scalable**: 100K+ customers, 1M+ readings/month
6. **Self-Serve**: Customer portal for bill viewing, meter reading submission

## Enterprise Vision
Transform MeterVerse from a billing application into an **Enterprise Utility Operating System** where billing is one module among many — contracts, tariffs, validation, collections, analytics, AI, and automation all exist as first-class domains.
